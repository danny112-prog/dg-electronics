FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all products genuine?

Yes! We source all products directly from authorized distributors and brands. Every item comes with a manufacturer warranty.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days nationwide. Express delivery (available on orders above ₦500,000) arrives within 1-2 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied, simply initiate a return through your account or contact our support team.

Do you offer warranty on products?

Yes, all products come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty. Premium devices include an extended 2-year warranty.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can also track your order from your account dashboard.
